ERNAKULAM TOURISM
Ernakulam refers to the eastern, mainland portion of the twin cities of Ernakulam-Kochi in central Kerala, India.
Located on the coast of the Arabian Sea, Ernakulam is a booming business metropolis. It is often referred to as the commercial capital of Kerala.
Ernakulam has been a part of human settlements since the Stone Age. Rock-cut caves and dolmens are still a part of this city. Ernakulam was ruled by the Chera Dynasty and later, by the Kingdom of Cochin (Perumpadapu Swaroopam).
The main tourist attractions in Ernakulam are the Hill Palace Museum, Madhavan Nayar Foundation, Kaladi, Kodanad, Chendamangalam, Malayatoor, Bhoothathankettu and Thattekkad Bird Sanctuary. Being home to numerous temples and churches, Ernakulam is a favorite among domestic tourists visiting Kerala.

Thattekkad Bird Sanctuary

Touted to be one of the first bird sanctuaries in Kerala, the place in Ernakulam is a haven for ornithologists and nature lovers! Famous ornithologist Salim Ali has even called it the richest bird sanctuary in India. So, you can imagine how exquisite the sanctuary is with myriad endangered species, including migratory birds. There are plantations of robust mahogany, rosewood, teak and rubber too that you can find here. Basically, it is a haven for nature enthusiasts. Do carry your cameras when visiting the alluring tourist destination in Ernakulam.

Cherai Beach

Something a lot of travellers opt for is a night of camping in the desert. The sky looks absolutely surreal at night, and you’ll have the time of your life. If you are visiting during the off-season, you’ll see lesser tourists and will be able to enjoy the amazing view to yourself. You will find quite a lot of camps that have decent washrooms and other basic amenities. Luxury tents are also a popularly chosen mode of accommodation and something you can opt for if you are travelling with your family or are on your honeymoon. Camping is one of the most sought-after things to do in Jodhpur.

Santa Cruz Basilica

Deemed to be one of the oldest cathedrals in India, Santa Cruz Basilica's popularity knows no bounds! The finesse in the architecture of the church along with the excellent brush strokes on historical paintings that can be found within the church, surely adds a sense of vintage charm to the property. It is as peaceful as it looks in every picture so you might want to add this to your list of places to visit in Ernakulam. Be amazed by the old-world charm of the Santa Cruz Basilica

Hill Palace

A prominent heritage museum, Hill Palace was constructed back in 1865 as a means to store the belongings of the Maharaja of Kochi. So, yes, you can find all the archaeological relics of Kerala along with personal belongings of the Maharaja of Kochi right here. Besides an interesting museum, Hill Palace also has a children's park and gardens attached to the property to add some fun spin to the place. Take your fam to the museum and learn more about Ernakulam!
